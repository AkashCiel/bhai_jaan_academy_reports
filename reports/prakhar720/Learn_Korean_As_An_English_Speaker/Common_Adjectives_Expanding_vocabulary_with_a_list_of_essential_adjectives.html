What Are Adjectives?

Adjectives are words that describe or modify nouns. They provide additional information about the noun, such as its qualities, quantities, or states. For instance, in English, we might describe a cat as "furry," "playful," or "small." Similarly, in Korean, adjectives serve the same purpose but have specific grammatical rules.
