Overview of the Two Number Systems

Korean has two distinct systems for counting: native Korean numbers and Sino-Korean numbers. Each system is used in different contexts, and understanding when to use each will enhance your fluency and comprehension in Korean.

    • Native Korean Numbers: Used for counting items, age, and certain expressions of quantity.
    • Sino-Korean Numbers: Derived from Chinese characters, these are used for dates, money, phone numbers, addresses, and more formal counting.

This report will provide a comprehensive overview of these systems, their applications, and their significance in Korean culture.
