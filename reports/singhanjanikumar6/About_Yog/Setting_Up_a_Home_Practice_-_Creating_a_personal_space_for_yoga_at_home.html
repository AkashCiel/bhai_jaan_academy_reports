3. Designing Your Space:

Once you have chosen the location, consider the following design elements:

    • Flooring: A flat, non-slip surface is ideal. If you have a hard floor, consider adding a yoga mat or carpet for comfort.
    • Decor: Incorporate elements that resonate with you, such as plants, artwork, or calming colors. These can evoke a sense of peace and inspiration.
    • Props and Accessories: Ensure your space is equipped with essential props like yoga mats, blocks, straps, and blankets. These tools will support your practice and enhance your learning.

Example 2: A Multi-Purpose Space
