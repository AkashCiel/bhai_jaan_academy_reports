Key Concepts of the Emotional Guidance Scale

What is the Emotional Guidance Scale?

The Emotional Guidance Scale, developed by Esther and Jerry Hicks, consists of a range of emotions that reflect different vibrational frequencies. The scale serves as a guide to help individuals identify their current emotional state and understand how it influences their experience of life. The EGS ranges from high-frequency emotions, such as joy and love, to low-frequency emotions, such as fear and despair.

The Structure of the Emotional Guidance Scale

The EGS can be visualized as a ladder of emotions:

    • Joy/Appreciation/Empowerment/Freedom/Love: The highest emotions that align with the highest vibrational frequency.
    • Passion: A strong emotional state that can drive you towards your goals.
    • Enthusiasm/Eagerness/Happiness: Positive emotions that signal alignment with desires.
    • Positive Expectation/Belief: A hopeful emotional state that fosters confidence in manifestation.
    • Optimism: A hopeful outlook that encourages positive thinking.
    • Hopefulness: A desire for better outcomes, though still slightly less powerful than optimism.
    • Contentment: A neutral emotional state that reflects acceptance.
    • Boredom: A low-energy state that indicates disengagement.
    • Pessimism: A negative outlook that can hinder manifestation.
    • Frustration/Irritation/Impatience: Emotions that reflect resistance to desired outcomes.
    • Overwhelm: A state of feeling burdened, which can stifle creativity and motivation.
    • Discouragement: A sense of defeat that can lead to inaction.
    • Fear/Grief/Depression/Despair/Powerlessness: The lowest states of emotion, indicating a significant disconnect from desires and intentions.

Understanding Emotional Vibrations

Each emotion on the EGS corresponds to a specific vibrational frequency. Higher emotions resonate at a frequency that attracts positive experiences, while lower emotions resonate at a frequency that can attract negativity. By recognizing where you currently stand on the scale, you can consciously work to elevate your emotional state to align with your desired outcomes.

The Importance of Emotional Awareness

Emotional awareness is the first step in using the EGS effectively. It involves recognizing your feelings without judgment and understanding their impact on your thoughts and actions. This awareness allows you to navigate your emotional landscape intentionally, moving toward higher vibrations that support your goals.
