Key Concepts and Definitions

What is Karmyog?

Karmyog is derived from two Sanskrit words: "karma," meaning action, and "yog," meaning union or path. It signifies a spiritual path that emphasizes performing one's duty with a sense of detachment from the fruits of those actions. This principle encourages individuals to focus on their responsibilities and engage in selfless service, embodying the essence of dharma (duty) without being weighed down by expectations or outcomes.

Leadership Defined

Leadership can be defined as the ability to influence and guide individuals or groups towards the achievement of goals. Effective leadership encompasses various styles and approaches, including transformational, transactional, and servant leadership. The modern understanding of leadership also emphasizes emotional intelligence and the ability to foster collaboration and resilience within teams.
