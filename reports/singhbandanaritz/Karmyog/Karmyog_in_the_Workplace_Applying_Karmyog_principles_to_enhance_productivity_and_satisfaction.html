2. Core Principles of Karmyog

To effectively apply Karmyog in the workplace, it's essential to understand its core principles:

    • Selflessness: Engaging in work for the benefit of the team and organization rather than for personal recognition or reward.
    • Detachment from Results: Focusing on the process of work rather than the end results, which can reduce stress and anxiety associated with performance pressure.
    • Duty (Dharma): Understanding and fulfilling one's responsibilities ethically and with commitment.
    • Equanimity: Maintaining a balanced mindset regardless of successes or failures, promoting resilience.
    • Continuous Improvement: Striving for personal and professional development, embracing feedback, and learning opportunities.
